Rasam Recipe

Ingredients

Rasam Powder (make at home or buy in store)

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder / red chillies 7
  • Garlic Cloves - 6

For Rasam:

  • Ripe Tomatoes - 6
  • Tamarind - Small Lemon Size
  • Salt - 1 Spoon
  • Turmeric - 3 Pinches

For Tempering

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder / red chillies 7
  • Garlic Cloves 6
