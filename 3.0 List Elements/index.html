Rasam Recipe

Rasam is a South Indian soup made with tomatoes, tamarind, and spices. It is typically served with rice and is known for its tangy flavor.

Ingredients

  • For Rasam Powder/ Get store bought Rasam Powder
    • Coriander seeds – 1 spoon
    • Cumin seeds – 1 spoon
    • Red chilies – 7
    • Curry leaves – 1/4 cup
    • Garlic cloves – 6
  • For rasam:
    • Ripe tomatoes – 9
    • Tamarind – small lemon size
    • Salt – 1 spoon
    • Turmeric – 3 pinches
  • For tempering:
    • Oil – 2 spoons
    • Tempering seeds – 1/2 spoon
    • Red chili – 1
  • For garnish:
    • Chopped coriander leaves – a handful

Instructions

  1. Prepare the rasam powder by dry roasting coriander seeds, cumin seeds, and red chilies.
  2. Add curry leaves and roast for a few more seconds.
  3. Blend the roasted spices with garlic cloves to a smooth powder.
  4. Blend ripe tomatoes with tamarind to a smooth puree.
  5. Heat oil in a pan, add tempering seeds and red chili, then add the rasam powder.
  6. Filter the tomato paste and mix it with water, salt, and turmeric.
  7. Boil for a few minutes and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
